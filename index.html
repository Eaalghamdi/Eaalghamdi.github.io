Hello! My name is Emad A. Alghamdi. I am a second-year PhD student in the Faculty of Languages and Linguistics at the University of Melbourne. I am also a Research Fellow in the Digital Studio at the same Uni. In my PhD work, I am working on developing a Machine Learning Model for Videotext Complexity. I am also working on developing a video playing interface for language learners.

Before starting my PhD, I was the founder and director of KAUx, a MOOC platform at King Abdulaziz University. I received an MA in English (TESOL) and MS in Educational Technology, from Missouri State University, USA.
